# **App Name**: Setu Guardian

## Core Features:

- Trusted Contact Management: Users can securely add, view, and manage a list of trusted emergency contacts for rapid communication.
- Live Journey Tracking: Travelers can initiate a journey, sharing their real-time location and progress with selected trusted contacts for peace of mind.
- Emergency SOS Alert: A prominent, one-tap feature to instantly send a distress alert, including the user's current location, to all designated trusted contacts.
- AI-Powered Emergency Message Composer: A generative AI tool that suggests and personalizes concise emergency messages for rapid dispatch, tailored to the user's location and inferred context.
- Safe Arrival/Departure Notifications: Enable automated or manual notifications to trusted contacts when a journey commences or safely concludes.
- On-Demand Support Requests: Allow users to send discrete requests for specific assistance (e.g., 'need a ride', 'help with delivery') to their trusted network.
- Virtual Companionship to Meeting Point: Users can invite a trusted contact to virtually accompany them to a specific meeting point, enabling temporary location sharing until arrival.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A confident and trustworthy deep blue (`#2280B3`), symbolizing security and clarity against a light background.
- Background color: A serene and calming off-white with a hint of blue (`#E7F0F3`), providing a clean canvas that subtly reinforces the primary hue.
- Accent color: A vibrant, energetic cyan (`#3DE8C0`) is utilized for critical alerts, calls to action, and highlighting moments of interaction, ensuring immediate user attention.
- All textual content will be rendered in 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional readability across devices and its modern, neutral aesthetic, reinforcing the app's clear communication.
- A suite of minimalist line icons will visually guide users, focusing on immediate comprehension for safety-critical functions and location services.
- A clean, intuitive mobile-first layout prioritizing ease of access to emergency functions and location sharing, designed for clarity under stressful or on-the-go conditions.
- Subtle, functional animations will provide immediate user feedback on actions like initiating journeys or sending alerts, enhancing interactivity without distracting from crucial information.
