# ShareRide: Key Features

- **Live GPS Navigation:** Real-time tracking with a dynamic "shrinking route" visual that updates as you move toward your destination.
- **Institutional Identity Verification:** Secure login and registration restricted to verified Student and Faculty roles via mobile OTP.
- **Smart Itinerary Broadcasting:** Instant sharing of comprehensive transit details including origin, destination, vehicle model, and AC status with your trusted circle.
- **Companion "Join" Requests:** Integrated passenger management allowing friends to request to join an active journey as travel companions.
- **3-Second SOS Protocol:** A specialized long-press interaction that triggers immediate system-wide alerts and AI-composed emergency SMS.
- **Multi-Recipient SMS Alerts:** Automatic dispatch of emergency messages with live Google Maps links to up to three pre-configured safety contacts.
- **AI-Driven Rendezvous:** Suggests optimal, safe meeting points along the route for companion pickups or check-ins.
- **End-to-End Secure Chat:** Private communication channel for real-time coordination between travelers and their verified network.
- **Cryptographic Event Logs:** Maintains secure, unalterable history of all safety incidents and alerts for institutional compliance.
- **Mobile-Optimized Interface:** High-visibility navigation arrows and one-handed UI controls designed for safe use during transit.
