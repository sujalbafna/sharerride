# Gemini Prompts for ShareRide Promotional Posters

Here are high-quality prompts you can use with Gemini (or Imagen) to generate stunning promotional visuals for ShareRide.

---

### 1. The "Safety Net" (Best for General Awareness)
> **Prompt:** "Create a professional, high-fidelity promotional poster for 'ShareRide', a university social safety app. The visual features a bird's-eye view of a modern city layout at night. A vibrant, glowing electric blue navigation line (the 'Shrinking Blue Line') is shown progressing toward a destination. Overlaying the map is a subtle, translucent digital mesh connecting several profile avatars, symbolizing a 'Trusted Circle' of friends watching over the journey. The lighting is cinematic with deep navy and teal tones. Professional 3D render style, clean, minimalist, and reassuring."

### 2. The "Campus Companion" (Focus on Students)
> **Prompt:** "A cinematic shot of a university campus at night with soft, warm streetlighting. In the foreground, a student is walking safely, holding a smartphone that displays a navigation app with a bright blue path. Faint, glowing shield icons appear in the air around the student, connected by thin digital lines representing a protective social network. The atmosphere is peaceful and secure. High-resolution photography style, 8k, bokeh background of campus buildings."

### 3. The "Rapid Response" (Focus on the SOS Feature)
> **Prompt:** "A sleek, close-up shot of a modern smartphone held in a hand. The screen shows the 'ShareRide' interface with a prominent, glowing red 'SOS' button that has a pulsing ripple effect. In the blurred background, a safety network of connected dots and lines is visible. The aesthetic is high-tech, urgent, and professional. Use a color palette of deep charcoal, vibrant red, and safety blue. Cyber-security aesthetic, photorealistic."

---

### How to use these prompts:
1. **Copy** one of the prompts above.
2. **Go to Gemini** (or any AI image generator like Midjourney or DALL-E).
3. **Paste** the prompt and add: "Generate an image based on this description in 9:16 aspect ratio for a mobile poster."
4. **Refine:** If you want text included, you can try adding: "Include the text 'SHARERIDE' in a bold, modern font at the top."
