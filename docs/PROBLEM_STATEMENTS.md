# ShareRide: Problem Statements

- Lack of real-time safety monitoring for students and faculty during solitary commutes.
- Existing navigation tools focus purely on directions rather than personal security oversight.
- Absence of a verified campus-only network for coordinating safe transit companions.
- Fragmented and insecure communication methods for sharing travel itineraries with trusted contacts.
- Delayed emergency response times due to manual SOS processes without instant location sharing.
- High risk of traveling with unverified individuals in non-institutional transit environments.
