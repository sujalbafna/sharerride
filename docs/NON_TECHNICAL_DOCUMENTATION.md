# ShareRide: Your Safety Companion (Non-Technical Overview)

## 1. Project Vision
ShareRide is a social-safety platform designed specifically for the MIT ADT University community. Our mission is to ensure that no student or faculty member ever has to travel alone. By leveraging the power of "Trusted Circles," we transform everyday transit into a secure, shared, and monitored experience.

---

## 2. The Problem We Solve
Many students face anxiety when traveling late at night, in unfamiliar areas, or using public transport. Standard GPS apps are for navigation, not safety. ShareRide bridges this gap by providing:
- **Visibility**: Your trusted friends can see exactly where you are in real-time.
- **Immediate Assistance**: A single-tap SOS protocol for emergencies.
- **Virtual Companionship**: The feeling of someone "watching over you" digitally.

---

## 3. Core Features for Users

### 🛡️ Trusted Friend Circle
You choose exactly who can see your location. You can add friends from the university network by name, ensuring your data is only shared with people you actually know and trust.

### 📍 Live Journey Tracking
When you start a journey, your selected friends receive a notification. They can follow your movement on a live map until you reach your destination safely.

### 🚗 Companion Mode (Ride-Sharing)
Traveling the same way? Broadcast your itinerary. Friends can request to join your transit, turning a solo trip into a safer group experience. You can even set meeting points along the way.

### 🚨 Rapid SOS Protocol
In case of danger, a single button press (or a 3-second hold for SMS) triggers a campus-wide safety protocol. Your precise GPS coordinates are instantly sent to your emergency contacts.

---

## 4. Why ShareRide is Different
Unlike generic tracking apps, ShareRide is:
1. **Community-Centric**: Built exclusively for university members.
2. **Privacy-First**: Your location is ONLY shared when you explicitly start a journey.
3. **Simple & Fast**: Designed for high-stress situations where every second counts.

---

## 5. User Journey: How to Use ShareRide
1. **Register**: Sign up with your university role (Student/Faculty).
2. **Build Your Circle**: Search for your friends and send connection requests.
3. **Set Emergency Contacts**: Add up to 3 mobile numbers for SMS alerts.
4. **Start a Journey**: Enter your destination and notify your friends.
5. **Travel with Peace of Mind**: Your circle monitors your progress until you "End Trip."

---

## 6. Conclusion
ShareRide isn't just an app; it's a digital safety net. It empowers the MIT ADT community to move freely and confidently, knowing that help is always just one tap away and their friends are always virtually by their side.
