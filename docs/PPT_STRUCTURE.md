## Slide 2: The Problem Statement
*   **The Issue:** Students and faculty often face safety concerns during late-night commutes or transit across campus and city areas.
*   **The Gap:** Standard navigation apps focus on directions, but not on "who" is watching over you.
*   **The Risk:** Solitary travel without a real-time emergency protocol or a verified network of friends.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution - ShareRide
*   **Core Concept:** A real-time safety hub that connects travelers with a verified circle of trusted friends.
*   **Mission:** To ensure that no one travels alone by providing virtual companionship and instant emergency dispatch.
*   **Philosophy:** "Safety through Community."

---

## Slide 4: Key Features (The Hub)
*   **Live Tracking:** GPS-enabled navigation with a "shrinking route" visual as you progress.
*   **Trusted Circle:** A searchable network of verified campus identities (Students/Faculty).
*   **Itinerary Broadcasting:** Detailed transit shares including Vehicle info, AC status, and available slots.
*   **Companion Rendezvous:** Friends can request to "Join" a journey or meet at AI-suggested safety points.

---

## Slide 5: Safety Protocols (Critical SOS)
*   **The 3-Second Hold:** A specialized UI interaction where long-pressing the SOS button prepares an emergency SMS broadcast.
*   **Multi-Recipient Alerts:** Automatic SMS dispatch to up to 3 pre-configured emergency contacts with live Google Maps links.
*   **System-Wide Broadcast:** Immediate notification to all connected friends within the ShareRide app.
*   **Security Event Logs:** Every alert is cryptographically logged for institutional compliance and safety review.

---

## Slide 6: Technical Stack
*   **Frontend:** Next.js 15 (App Router), React 19, Tailwind CSS.
*   **UI Components:** ShadCN UI (Radix Primitives) for a professional, accessible interface.
*   **Backend:** Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) for real-time data sync.
*   **Mapping:** Google Maps Platform (Directions API, Places API, Geometry Library).
*   **AI Integration:** Genkit for composing emergency messages and analyzing optimal meeting points.

---

## Slide 7: User Journey Flow
1.  **Identity:** User registers as Student/Faculty via verified mobile OTP.
2.  **Network:** Build a "Friend Circle" by searching campus profiles.
3.  **Broadcast:** User initializes a journey from A to B with vehicle details.
4.  **Transit:** Friends receive notifications and can "Track Live" or "Join" as passengers.
5.  **Completion:** The journey ends, notifying the network of a safe arrival.

---

## Slide 8: UI/UX Highlights
*   **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for one-handed use during travel.
*   **Visual Navigation:** High-visibility navigation arrows and custom map markers.
*   **Instant Feedback:** Real-time badges and pulsating indicators for active transits.
*   **Simplified SOS:** A high-contrast, clutter-free emergency interface.

## Slide 9: Future Roadmap

*   **Institutional Integration:** Direct link to Campus Security/Police Outposts.
